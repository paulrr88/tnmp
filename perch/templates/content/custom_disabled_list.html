Instructions:

This is the list of disabled facilities that the church offers. Sections can be added or deleted as needed. Each section has a header with a bullet point list within it which can be edited.

To add a section of bullet points with a header: Click "Save & add another" at the bottom of the page as usual.

To add a bullet point: Click "Add an item" in the lower right corner underneath the bottom-most bullet point in a section.

To delete a bullet point: Mouse over the number next to a bullet point's text below to get a red X to appear, click the red X to delete the bullet point.

To reorder bullet points: Mouse over the number as above, but instead click and hold on the three lines underneath the red X and drag your mouse down/up to reorder.

To delete an entire section: Find the red "Delete" button to the top right of the section you want to delete, and click Delete in the "Are you sure?" box that appears.

As ever for any of this to take effect you need to click Save Changes at the bottom. If you make a mistake just click Cancel instead.
